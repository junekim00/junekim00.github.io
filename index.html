Who Are We

USC Delta Phi Kappa is an Asian-interest sorority chartered exclusively at the University of Southern California. As an organization dedicated to spreading friendship and service throughout our university, we strive to form lasting bonds through scholastic achievement, leadership, and philanthropic dedication.

History

In the spring of 1960, eleven Asian co-eds of the University of Southern California organized the need to foster sisterhood among Asian women and to stimulate an intercultural exchange among themselves and their fellow students at USC.
