Fall 2020 Information


Involvement Fair

Come see us at the USC involvement fair! Dates are TBA. We will have our own table our wooden letters, as well as our own individual lettered apparel. Feel free to ask anything about Deltas or Asian Greek life in general!


Discover AGC

Every year, AGC holds a information session for anyone who has interest in joining Asian Greek life. As always, Deltas will be their to present our experiences and answer any questions!



Rush

Due to the current circumstances with Coronavirus, potential rush events are on hold until further notice. We will be updating our website with additional information as USC reveals information about the upcoming semester, so please check again at a later date!


After Rush

After rush, we will remain in contact with all of the girls who joined us at any of our rush events with further information. At the end of each rush event, we will announce the date of bid extensions, so please pay attention and save the date on your calendar! You will have 24 hours to accept or decline your bid.
